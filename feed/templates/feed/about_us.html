My Vision about this blog

Dearest Chemistry Family

I write this message with conviction in the belief that “the future lying ahead of us requires healthy collaborations” in order to succeed in our professional lives and elsewise as well. I reckon most of us indeed have this realization.

In the fast-moving world of Instagram stories, it’s hard to catch up with everyone and often times we are not even aware of the expertise of our peers.

It is with this thought I intend to work on this blog with the purpose to bring the student community of the Chemistry Department, IIT Bombay on the same page about their whereabouts in their professional lives. In addition to that this blog will contain course reviews, project reviews, POR reviews, resources to help the student community.

This space also intends to provide neutral space to students with an inclination in writing as well.

At last, I in hope that this blog brings like-minded people together and new “bonds” are formed eventually.

Harshvardhan Nigam

Chief Editor
